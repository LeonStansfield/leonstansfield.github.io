Some other projects

by Leon Stansfield

Posted on 4th May 2023

A collection of other smaller work I have done!

Tiny Church

A quick scene I made in an afternoon of a tiny church in a field!

Racoon

A racoon character I modelled, textured, rigged and animated for my YouTube videos! He continues to be the face of my videos today!

Well warrior

A game I made for the brackeys 2023.1 game jam! The game features fast paced top down shooter gameplay, set in a labyrinth of procedural rooms. You play as a warrior in search for the well of peace. Upon death you respawn, keeping all currency, which you can use at a shop as well as your purchased upgrades. Check it out here.

Combat prototype

A small combat prototype that I worked on for a few weeks, designed to be similar to Hyper Light Drifter in 3D. The project was eventually abandoned as it was when I was starting university, but it was good fun to work on!

Grounded

A game built for the bullet hell jam 2022, coming in at a hot 22 overall (out of 296). The game features fast paced and satisfying bullet hell gameplay with a vibrant art style. It is available on the web but is best played on a desktop. Play it here!

Material work

Some practice making materials with different masks, and imperfections.
